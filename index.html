Here are some facts about Nelson Mandela:

  • Full name - Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela
  • 1918 -Born into Madiba clan in the village of Mvezo, in the Eastern Cape
  • 1930 - He was 12 years old, his father died and the young Rolihlahla became a ward of Jongintaba at the Great Place in Mqhekezweni
  • Miss Mdingane, gave him the name Nelson
  • Bachelor of Arts degree at the University College of Fort Hare
  • 1941 - Ran away to Johannesburg
  • 19-43 - Completed BA through the University of South Africa and went back to Fort Hare for his graduation
  • 1962 - Started studying again through the University of London after his imprisonment
  • 1989 - while in the last months of his imprisonment, he obtained an LLB through the University of South Africa
  • 1944 - joined the African National Congress. Married Walter Sisulu’s cousin, Evelyn Mase, a nurse
  • 1952 - chosen as the National Volunteer-in-Chief of the Defiance Campaign with Maulvi Cachalia as his deputy
  • 1958 - Divorced
  • 1994 - He was inaugurated as South Africa’s first democratically elected President
  • 2013 - He died at his home in Johannesburg
